Create your story

The best way to improve your imagination, practice your English and have fun with your friends!

How to Play:

Easy:Choose only 3 pictures and create your story.

Medium:You have to use all the pictures in order to create your story.

Hard:You have to use all the pictures in order to create your story but you cannot see all the pictures at once.
First you'll see just the introduction pictures, them you can see the development and later the conclusion.
